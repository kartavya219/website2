FAQs

1. What are the courses offered by Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology University at Patiala Campus?

Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology University offers undergraduate programs leading to a Bachelor's degree in Engineering and Technology, Post graduate program in Engineering Science and Arts, and Doctoral program.
For more details, visit:
• UG - http://thapar.edu/index.php/admissions/under-graduate
• PG and PhD - http://thapar.edu/index.php/admissions/post-graduate



2. What is the eligibility criterion for admission to a program?

The details about the eligibility criteria for various programs are available at:
• UG - https://forms.thapar.edu/ug-eligibility
• PG - https://forms.thapar.edu/pg-eligibility
• PhD - https://forms.thapar.edu/phd-eligibility



3. How can I apply to various programs offered by Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology University?

You can apply to various programs offered by TIETU through an online application form available at website of Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology University. Please check for the last date of application which can be confirmed at the link: http://thapar.edu/images/admission2016/Important%20DatesInformation.pdf



4. What is the fee component of various courses offered by Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology University?

The fee details can be browsed on official website of TIETU through the respective program pages which carry the same. For details visit the link: http://thapar.edu/images/admission2016/Tuition%20fee%20and%20other%20dues.pdf



5. When is the admission fee to be paid?

The admission fee will be paid after allocation of provisional seat during the online counselling within a stipulated time as mentioned in our brochure. During the offline/in-person counselling the fee will have to be deposited on the spot. No admission will be given without payment of full fees.



6. What are the total numbers of seats available for Foreign Nationals/NRI students?

A total 15% of the sanctioned strength (over and above of the total seats) in a program are reserved for Foreign Nationals and NRI students. For more details, please visit the link: http://thapar.edu/images/admission2016/Admission%20for%20FN-NRI%20Candidates.pdf



7. Is there any entrance exam for admission under FN/NRI category?

There is no entrance examination for students seeking admission under FN/NRI category to any program.

For UG Programs
The admission shall be on the basis of PCM (in Aggregate) in 10+2 level or equivalent with minimum of 60% marks.

For PG Programs
For admissions to Masters Programs a minimum of 60% marks in the qualifying examination is required.

'NRI' candidates are those who have passed the qualifying examination as mentioned in the eligibility from abroad. They are required to produce Passport at the time of admission.

Note: Candidates seeking admission under 'wards of person working in Gulf' must have passed 10+2 from Gulf.

'FN' candidates are those who are citizen of a country other than India. Persons of Indian origin (PIO) and wards of persons working in Gulf and South Asia also come under this category. 5% seats are reserved for wards of person working in Gulf and South Asia. All FN candidates are required to deposit their endorsed student visa to TIETU. The VISA should be valid for the prescribed duration of the program.

Details of other eligibility criteria is available at:
• UG - https://forms.thapar.edu/ug-eligibility
• PG - https://forms.thapar.edu/pg-eligibility
• PhD - https://forms.thapar.edu/phd-eligibility
